Elle Cheng Li Ling
flatness

A webcam-filmed homage to the four walls and my skin. In a time of great change and isolation. 

Self-written, directed and performed. Submission for the Be Still Media Art Competition 2020. Finalist.

Are You Listening?

"A generation frozen in formulaic notions, another generation born into exhaustion. How might they broach a topic taboo to one but vital to the other? This play briefly explores an intergenerational relationship to consider attitudes towards death, grief, letting go and living."
Elle plays 'Death' in "Are You Listening?" written by Melissa Peh as part of the 2021 NUS Stage Internal Productions (Lost in Translation).

Girl of the Mud

On growing up, the possibility of growing apart, but the common foundation, shared material that will always bind them.
As part of NUS Build-A-Bridge project, a short film based on a photograph taken by Ritichelle Mantiquilla. Elle performs the voiceover. Filmed and directed by Elle along with Melissa Peh and Michelle Simon Hariff who were also the performers in this short film.

Medusa with the Head of Poseidon

(Selected Scenes)

A contemporary story alongside the Greek tale of Medusa, more than just the monstrous gorgon so widely known. A story of reclamation.
Elle plays 'Medusa' in 'Medusa with the Head of Poseidon" written by Christian Priyanka as part of the 2020 NUS Stage Internal Productions (The Odyssey). 2020.

"Romeo and Juliet"

(Selected Scenes)

A Singaporean twist to the classic tale of Shakespeare's "Romeo and Juliet". 
Elle plays 'Nancy' or the 'Nurse' in "Romeo and Juliet" written, filmed and directed by Melissa Peh and Rebecca Chiang. 2021.

Substitue Teacher (Remake)

A substitute teacher seems relentlessly unable to pronounce the names of his students.
Elle plays 'Hannah' in "Substitue Teacher (Remake) as part of a project by film students at the Nanyang Academy of Fine Art. 2019.

3 Words

A short film on loss and disillusionment.
Elle plays Gary's girlfriend in "3 Words" as part of a project by film students at the Nanyang Academy of Fine Art. 2019.

Under the Rainbow: Natalie Wood is Dead | Duologue

(Rehearsal, Selected Scenes)

Natalie Wood, acclaimed actress, attempts to explain her traumatic experiences on set to her mother, who only displays apathy.
Elle plays "Natalie Wood" for an extract from Philip Kan Gotanda's "Under the Rainbow: Natalie Wood is Dead" (2005), a duologue as part of her A'Level Theatre Studies and Drama Examinations. 2018.

The World of Extreme Happiness | Monologue

(Rehearsal, Selected Scenes)

Sunny Li, a peasant girl who moved to the city in search for a better life, only finds this dream limited to that and fed only by lies.
Elle plays "Sunny Li" for an extract from Frances Ya-Chu Cowhig's "The World of Extreme Happiness" (2013), a monlogue as part of her A'Level Theatre Studies and Drama Examinations. 2018.
