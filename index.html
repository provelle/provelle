Elle Cheng Li Ling

hello! my name's elle.

I am an actor and a work in progress. As you traipse these pages, you will discover a little more about who I am and the art that I love to do.

As an artist, much of my interests and pursuits so far lie in exploring the human, within contexts either extraordinary or exceedingly mundane. I enjoy spectacles, magic and mythology, but I also love observing habitual tics, micro-aggressions, the slip-of-the-tongue and glanced-over. How mountains! could be made out of molehills.
I believe the human body to be an instinctual vessel that conveys more than we could ever say, and I think that tethers us to the plants and all the other animals. I draw many lessons and inspirations from the natural world, and currently work to convey them through performance.
